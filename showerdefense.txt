Title: Shower Defense

single 2d map
set path for mobs
click to build towers around the path
20 lives
10 waves, 15 per wave, 10th is boss
mobs are spiders, wasps (air units), toaster, snakes, scorpions, scary animals, ball of hair
towers are shampoo, conditioner, bodywash, ice, showerheads, drains (trap)
currency is soap
